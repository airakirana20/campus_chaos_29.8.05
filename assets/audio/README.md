Drop your music and sound files into these folders and keep the names below.

Music tracks:
- `music/home.ogg`
- `music/loading.ogg`
- `music/day.ogg`
- `music/dream.ogg`
- `music/week_win.ogg`
- `music/week_lose.ogg`

Sound effects:
- `sfx/ui_start.wav`
- `sfx/mission_accept.wav`
- `sfx/mission_complete.wav`
- `sfx/mission_fail.wav`
- `sfx/day_end.wav`
- `sfx/dream_start.wav`
- `sfx/dream_success.wav`
- `sfx/dream_fail.wav`
- `sfx/powerup.wav`
- `sfx/loading_done.wav`

The game will also accept `.ogg` or `.mp3` for most of these files, but `.ogg` for music and `.wav` for short SFX is the safest setup in `pygame`.
